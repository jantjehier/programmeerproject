Best album selling music artists

On this website you can learn more about the best album selling music artists.The bar chart shows the number of albums sold by a music artist or band. The colors show if a band is still active or not. When you click on the bar chart you will see a timeline and a pie chart. The timeline shows which members have been in the band at a certain time and when the band started. The pie chart shows for the albums that where sold more than 20 million times, the percentage of the total albumsales. You can also search for a band with the search function. This will also change the pie chart and timeline.
